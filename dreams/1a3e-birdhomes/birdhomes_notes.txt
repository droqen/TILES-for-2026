	i had a sudden realization
	when i saw and thought ab-
	out birds and making homes
	-- how frightening it must
	be to create a home in any
	place at all, not knowing,
	for sure, that it will re-
	main as is it is, and not,
	as birds are not, knowing,
	regardless of the state of
	things at the time of home
	making, whether or not you
	will have a say in changes
	unknown to come or none at
	all.
	
	i am sure i will, utterly,
	fail to capture this sens-
	ation in today's game. but
	one must begin to create a
	home somewhere.
	
	droqen.
