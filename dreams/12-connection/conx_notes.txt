	Tarot card for this project:
	e. three of cups

	three friends meet, clinking cups together, yet residing still in their separate worlds: a rocky shoreline, a decorated town, a green field. in each world, flowers. the three worlds are overlapping, juxtaposed on top of a sky blue background with a cloud drifting by. many of my friendships have been formed and maintained across the internet, our worlds' separation literal. that's what i see when i look at this card, but there is a way in which every friendship and relationship involves bridging an unbridgeable gap, we all in our separate worlds. but it's a very joyful card! we choose to connect, even across worlds.

		"we choose to connect."
